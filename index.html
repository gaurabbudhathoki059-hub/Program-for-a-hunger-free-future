Hunger

Hunger is a serious global problem where people do not get enough nutritious food to live a healthy life. Millions of people, especially children, suffer from hunger every day. At the same time, large amounts of food are wasted due to poor management, lack of awareness, and inefficient distribution systems.

This problem is not only about lack of food, but about how food is distributed. By using technology, we can create simple systems that connect excess food with people who need it, helping to reduce both hunger and food waste.
